About

In 2015 joined as a Research Fellow the Visual Information Laboratory, led by Prof. David Bull, within the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University of Bristol. Before that, I received my Ph.D. in 2014 at the Computer Science and Engineering Department, University of Ioannina (Greece). I obtained the M.Eng. from Electrical Engineering and Computer Technology Department, University of Patras, and my M.Sc. in Computer Engineering and Informatics Department, University of Patras (Greece).
